Welcome to Boracay Island

Boracay is a tropical paradise located in the Philippines. It is a small island in the Western Visayas region, known for its stunning white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Boracay attracts tourists from all over the world and is a popular destination for beach lovers and water sports enthusiasts.

Island Highlights

  • White Beach: The most famous beach on the island, White Beach, stretches over four kilometers and offers breathtaking sunset views.
  • Puka Shell Beach: Located on the northern part of the island, Puka Beach is known for its coarser sand and beautiful Puka shells.
  • Water Sports: Boracay offers a wide range of water sports activities, including snorkeling, scuba diving, kiteboarding, and parasailing.
  • Mount Luho: For a panoramic view of Boracay, visitors can hike up Mount Luho, the highest point on the island.
